UPSC – EPFO 2025 Online Form

The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) invites online applications for the post of Enforcement Officer (EO) and Assistant Provident Fund Commissioner (APFC) in EPFO. This is a golden opportunity to work in a reputed central government organization.

Important Dates

  • Application Begin: 07 August 2025
  • Last Date to Apply: 26 August 2025
  • Fee Payment Last Date: 26 August 2025 (Till 23:59 hrs)
  • Correction Window: 27 – 31 August 2025
  • Exam Date: To be announced

Application Fee

  • General / OBC / EWS: ₹25
  • SC / ST / PwBD / Female: ₹0 (Exempted)

Eligibility

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Any Stream from a Recognized University
  • Desirable: Degree in Law/Integrated Five-year Law/LLB/MBA/PGDM/CS/CA/CMA

Age Limit

  • Minimum Age: 21 Years
  • Maximum Age: 30 Years
  • Age relaxation as per UPSC rules

Vacancy Details

  • Enforcement Officer (EO): 418
  • Assistant PF Commissioner (APFC): 159
